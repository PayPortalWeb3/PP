# $PP Tokenomics

> Contract Address: `Axa4Ebtf7Q2yvyQ8dMidu6Qf17yZtsutAKEuKgdKpump`

## Overview

$PP is the native token of the PayPortal ecosystem. It provides utility for both customers making payments and merchants accepting them.

---

## Current Token Utility

### Customer Benefits

| Mechanism | Benefit |
|-----------|---------|
| **Pay with $PP** | 10% discount on any payment |
| **Hold $PP** | Up to 50% discount based on tier |

#### Holder Discount Tiers

| Tier | $PP Balance | Discount |
|------|-------------|----------|
| Diamond | 1,000,000+ | 50% |
| Platinum | 500,000+ | 30% |
| Gold | 100,000+ | 15% |
| Silver | 10,000+ | 5% |

## Planned Tokenomics

### Merchant Tiers

Merchants hold $PP to unlock premium features.

| Tier | $PP Required | Features |
|------|--------------|----------|
| **Free** | 0 | Basic payments, admin panel, webhooks |
| **Pro** | 10,000 | + Analytics dashboard, + 10 webhook retries, + Email notifications |
| **Business** | 50,000 | + Multi-user admin, + Custom branding, + 10x API rate limit |
| **Enterprise** | 250,000 | + White-label (no PayPortal branding), + Priority support, + Custom integrations |

### Protocol Fee + Burn

Small fee on payments with deflationary mechanism.

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Base Fee | 0.1% on all payments |
| Burn Rate | 50% of fees burned |
| Treasury | 50% of fees to development |

#### Fee Reduction for $PP Holders

| $PP Held | Fee Reduction | Effective Fee |
|----------|---------------|---------------|
| 0 | 0% | 0.10% |
| 10,000 | 25% | 0.075% |
| 50,000 | 50% | 0.05% |
| 250,000 | 75% | 0.025% |
| 1,000,000+ | 100% | 0% (free) |

### Revenue Sharing (Staking)

Stake $PP to earn a share of protocol fees.

- 80% of collected fees distributed to stakers
- Distributions occur weekly
- Earnings proportional to stake size

#### Example (at $1M monthly volume)

| Your Stake | % of Pool | Monthly Earning |
|------------|-----------|-----------------|
| 10,000 $PP | 0.1% | $0.80 |
| 100,000 $PP | 1% | $8.00 |
| 1,000,000 $PP | 10% | $80.00 |

#### At Scale ($100M monthly volume)

| Your Stake | Monthly Earning |
|------------|-----------------|
| 100,000 $PP | $800 |
| 1,000,000 $PP | $8,000 |
